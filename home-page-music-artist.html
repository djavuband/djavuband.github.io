About Evelyn

Evelyn Burke is a London-based creative artist. She’s inspired by well known modern Jazz artists. Evelyn’s exquisite vocal has been recognized along Uk and Europe, and been discovered by most popular music magazines and publications. Evelyn has performed as a solo artist and with her new band across Europe and now starting a tour in USA.

I simply love music! Performing on a stage, transferring all that positive energy to my public is like a dream come true. A feeling when someone sings your song is epic. I’m glad to be able to share that feeling and enjoy it in my everyday job and life!
